This is the Archer Products page for EchoTribe / MommyandMeAI. 
Here is how the system is designed to work and what the expected

────────────────────────────────────────
HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS
────────────────────────────────────────
Steph is an Amazon affiliate creator. We have her earnings CSV 
(586 ASINs she has sold). We cross-reference those ASINs against 
two brand affiliate networks she has access to — Archer (113,835 
product catalog via CSV) and Levanta (537 accessible products via 
live API). The goal is to identify which of her proven products 
are available at higher commission rates through these networks, 
and to discover new products from those same brands she could 
promote.

The matching has two passes:
1. Direct ASIN match — earnings ASIN exists in Archer or Levanta
2. Brand expansion — earnings ASIN matched a brand, so ALL products 
   from that brand in Archer/Levanta are surfaced as opportunities

The scan writes matched_asins.json and scan_meta.json. The UI reads 
from matched_asins.json to render the matched grid.

────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT HAS BEEN BUILT
────────────────────────────────────────
- asin_match_scan() in product_api.py runs both passes
- ArcherNetworkMatcher reads from the Archer catalog CSV (113,835 ASINs)
- LevantaNetworkMatcher calls the live Levanta API, caches to 
  data/network_cache_levanta.json, falls back to cache on failure
- Levanta brand names require resolving brandId via the /brands 
  endpoint since product responses only return brandId (UUID)
- Every record in matched_asins.json should have: networks[], 
  archer_matched bool, levanta_matched bool, archer_brand_match bool
- Images: Levanta API returns imageUrl per product. Archer images 
  come from SQLite image_encoded_string (mostly empty). The cache 
  should store image URLs from Levanta so matched records can use them.
- Frontend has network toggle (Archer / Levanta / Both) that filters 
  the matched grid based on those boolean fields

────────────────────────────────────────
EXPECTED OUTCOMES
────────────────────────────────────────
After a successful scan the data should show:
- total_asins: ~2,645 (586 earnings + ~2,059 brand-expanded)
- archer matched: ~2,062 (3 direct + brand expansion from Jay Franco, 
  SpaceAid, Wise Owl Outfitters)
- levanta matched: > 0 if any of those 3 brands have Levanta products

The matched_asins.json currently on disk has 217 records with NO 
networks, archer_matched, or levanta_matched fields — it is stale 
and needs to be regenerated by running the scan.
